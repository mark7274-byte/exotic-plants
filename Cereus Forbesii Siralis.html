Cereus Forbesii - Description

About Cereus Forbesii

Cereus Forbesii, also known as the night-blooming cereus, is a species of cactus native to South America. It's famous for its stunning white flowers that bloom at night.

Care Instructions

  • Light: Full sunlight
  • Watering: Water moderately, allowing soil to dry out between waterings
  • Temperature: Prefers temperatures between 50-85°F (10-29°C)
