Monstera Deliciosa - Description

About Monstera Deliciosa

The Monstera Deliciosa, also known as the Swiss cheese plant, is a tropical climbing vine native to Central America. Its distinctive leaves have natural holes, giving it a unique appearance.

Care Instructions

  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight
  • Watering: Water when the top 1-2 inches of soil are dry
  • Temperature: Prefers temperatures between 65-85°F (18-29°C)
