Ficus Elastica 'Tineke' - Description

About Ficus Elastica 'Tineke'

The Ficus Elastica 'Tineke' is a stunning variety of the rubber plant, known for its beautiful variegated leaves with creamy white and green patterns. Native to Southeast Asia, it’s an attractive choice for indoor spaces due to its glossy, bold foliage.

Care Instructions

  • Light: Prefers bright, indirect light but can tolerate moderate light
  • Watering: Water when the top inch of soil is dry, avoid waterlogging
  • Temperature: Thrives in temperatures between 60-85°F (16-29°C)
